Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how G² Yoyodex ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information about how you interact with our website, including:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on the site
  • Search terms you use
  • Filters and sorting options you select
  • Yoyos you mark as favorites or owned
  • Yoyo details you view

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Analyze how our website is used
  • Improve the user experience
  • Make informed decisions about future updates

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze usage data. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our website. The information generated by these cookies about your use of our website (including your IP address) is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

Google may use this information to evaluate your use of our website, compile reports on website activity for us, and provide other services related to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your preferences, such as which yoyos you've marked as favorites or owned. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Your Choices

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

You can clear your local storage data at any time by clearing your browser cache and cookies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
